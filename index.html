Ran Ji (Sara)

Hi! I’m Ran Ji (also known as Sara), a Psychology major at UC Davis, passionate about child development, early autism detection, and inclusive education. I currently work with the MIND Institute and ASPIRE Program on projects involving neurodiverse populations, including autism and ADHD.

I’m particularly interested in how children understand emotions, attribute intentions to AI and humans, and construct meaning through interaction. I’ve also served as a special education classroom assistant, where I supported neurodiverse learners aged 6 to 21.

Research Interests

  • Early autism detection and family-based intervention
  • Children's theory of mind and attribution of agency
  • AI in education and human-robot interaction
  • Social-emotional development in neurodiverse children
  • Inclusive curriculum design and special education practices

Selected Projects

  • Do Children Attribute Minds to AI Voices?
    An experimental study examining how children interpret emotions and intentions from AI vs. human speech, using behavioral tasks and interview data.
    👉 Focus: Attribution bias, emotion recognition, neurotypical vs. autistic children.
  • Secret Bases and Child Autonomy
    A creative project exploring how children create "secret bases" and how such spaces support their autonomy, imagination, and social connection.
    👉 Tools: Drawing tasks, interviews, cultural comparisons.
  • Schoolwide Emotion Support System
    Developing a comprehensive emotion support model for a 9-year consistent school in Shanghai, integrating teacher training, student space design, and home-school collaboration.
    👉 Emphasis: Proactive intervention, emotional regulation, inclusive practices.

季然（Sara）

你好！我是季然（Sara），就读于加州大学戴维斯分校心理学专业，专注于儿童发展、早期自闭症识别与包容性教育研究。 我目前在 MIND 研究所 与 ASPIRE 本科生科研项目 参与多个项目，研究对象包括自闭症、ADHD 等神经多样性群体。

我关注儿童如何理解情绪、如何将意图归因于 AI 或真人、以及他们如何在互动中构建意义。 我曾担任特殊教育课堂助理，服务对象包括 6 至 21 岁的神经多样性学生。

研究兴趣

  • 早期自闭症识别与家庭干预
  • 儿童的心理理论与意图归因
  • 教育中的人工智能与人机互动
  • 神经多样性儿童的社会情绪发展
  • 包容性课程设计与特殊教育实务

项目介绍

  • 儿童会将“意识”归因给 AI 吗？
    一项实验研究，探讨儿童如何从 AI 与人类语言中解读情绪与意图，使用行为任务与访谈资料。
    👉 关键词：归因偏差、情绪识别、典型发展 vs. 自闭症儿童
  • 秘密基地与儿童自主性
    一项创意项目，探索儿童如何创造“秘密基地”，以及这些空间如何支持他们的自主性、想象力与社交连接。
    👉 方法：绘画任务、访谈、文化比较
  • 校园情绪支持系统
    为上海某九年一贯制学校开发情绪支持系统，整合教师培训、学生空间设计与家校合作。
    👉 重点：主动性干预、情绪调节、包容性教育实践
