Ran Ji (Sara)

Hi! I'm Ran (Sara) Ji, a Psychology student at UC San Diego. Before UCSD, I studied Psychology and Cognitive Science at UC Davis. I am drawn to questions about how children and intelligent systems learn, reason, misunderstand, and grow.

My recent work sits between developmental psychology, child mental health, and AI cognition. I spend time with the VBVR Team, Grow AI, the ASPIRE Program, and the MIND Institute at UC Davis.

Things I Think About

  • How AI systems reason through videos, scenes, and social perspectives
  • How confidence, uncertainty, and self-awareness appear in vision–language models
  • How early gestures and language shape child development
  • How culture affects mental health, stigma, and help-seeking
  • How classrooms can become kinder places for emotional expression
